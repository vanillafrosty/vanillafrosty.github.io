About

Hello! I'm a software engineer with a focus on automation and data analysis.

I work on creating APIs, database ETLs, and job queues. I am experienced in frontend development in React and SASS/SCSS. On the devOps side, I've worked with AWS deployments and tools such as Jenkins/Chef.

My professional hobbies include asking good questions and shipping clean code. I have a soft spot for concise, informative documentation.
